# Weekly Project Reflection Week 1

## Accomplishments
Provide a concise overview of completed tasks and identify any remaining work or follow-up items.

## Skills Learned
List the new skills, tools, concepts, or methodologies you explored or improved during the project.

## Issues Faced
Document any challenges, errors, or obstacles encountered, along with how you resolved them—or how you plan to address them in the future.

## Deficiency Reflection
Describe any knowledge gaps, misunderstandings, or areas where additional preparation would have been helpful before starting.

## Incorrect Strategy
Identify specific changes you would make to your approach, workflow, or planning in similar future projects.

## Correct Strategy
Highlight strengths, effective decisions, or habits that contributed positively to the project and that you want to carry forward.

## Tools Used
List all tools, libraries, frameworks, languages, documentation, videos, and other resources that supported the project.

## Time Breakdown
Estimate which tasks or phases required the most time and evaluate whether the time allocation was appropriate.

## Final Reflection
Summarize the overall experience. Consider the project’s success, what it contributed to your growth, and whether it has potential for future expansion or improvement.
